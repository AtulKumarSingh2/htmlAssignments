Benifits of Kidney Beans:-

Kidney beans cooked by boiling are 67% water, 23% carbohydrates, 9% protein, and contain negligible amount of fat. In a 100 gram reference amount, cooked kidney beans provide 127 kcal, and are a rich source (20% or more of the Daily Value) of protein, folate (33% DV), iron (22% DV), and phosphorus (20% DV), with moderate amounts (10-19% DV) of thiamin, copper, magnesium, and zinc (11-14% DV).

Benifits Of Tofu:-

Tofu is made from soybean curds. It is naturally gluten-free and low in calories. It is also a rich source of amino acids, iron, calcium and other nutrients. Tofu contains eight essential amino acids and is makes for excellent vegetarian food from a nutritional and health perspective. Tofu can be integrated with almost all types of dishes and flavours. A half cup of tofu contains about 10 grams of dietary protein.
